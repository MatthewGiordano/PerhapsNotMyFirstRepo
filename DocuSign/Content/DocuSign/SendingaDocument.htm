Sending a Document

DocuSign allows you to send one or more documents to one or more people for signature.

 

Recipients can be inside or outside of Martin Marietta.

 

In addition, recipients DO NOT need to have a DocuSign account in order to view and sign a document that you send to them.

 

If you repeatedly send similar documents, or send them to the same people, you can create a DocuSign Template (Opens a new tab) to save time.

 

 

Sharing Documents Needing Signatures

To send an Envelope containing documents in need of signatures:

  1. Launch DocuSign

  2. Wait for the DocuSign Home screen to be displayed

     

  3. Click the "START" drop-down in the middle of the screen

  4. Select "Send an Envelope"

     

  5. OR Drag and Drop a document into the same document area on the Home screen

     

  6. Wait for the "Add Documents to the Envelope" screen to open

  7. Ensure the correct documents are attached by viewing the thumbnails

  8. Attach additional documents by selecting the "Upload" button

     

  9. Add recipient(s) to the envelope. Accomplish this by:

    • Typing in their information

    • Clicking the name field and using the directory

       

  10. Add further recipients by clicking "ADD RECIPIENT"

  11. Add additional recipients

     

    NOTE: You will NEED the recipient's name and email address.

     

    NOTE: The "Action" field will default to "Needs to Sign" but can be changed to other actions. If you have multiple recipients, you can also set the order that they need to sign by clicking the "Set Signing Order" button then entering the signing order.

     

     

     

  12. Add a subject line and message to be included in the notification email received by recipients

     

  13. Navigate to the "ACTIONS" drop-down near the top of the page to

    • Save the Envelope to continue later

    • Send the Envelope NOW (Do not use until envelope is COMPLETE)

    • Discard the Envelope

     

  14. Click "ADVANCED OPTIONS" in the top right-hand corner

     

  15. Add any desired advanced features

  16. Select "SAVE"

  17. Click the "NEXT" button

     

  18. Add locations requiring initials, signatures, and supplemental text boxes by dragging the item from the Standard Fields panel on the left of the selected document.

     

  19. Click "RECIPIENT PREVIEW" to view what the document will look like for recipients

     

    NOTE: A preview screen will open and display the document as the recipient will see. The person's name and the date were automatically filled in by DocuSign.

     

  20. Click the "X" in the upper right-hand corner to close the preview

  21. Click "SEND" to send the document to the recipients

     

     

    NOTE: Recipients will now receive an email informing them that a document has been sent to them. Recipients will be able to view and complete any actions by clicking the "REVIEW DOCUMENT" button.

     

  22. Await the completion confirmation email indicating that the recipient has completed all required fields

     

    NOTE: Navigate to the "Manage" under "Sent" to see the status of the sent item.
