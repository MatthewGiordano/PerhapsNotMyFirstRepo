Signing a Document

Recipients DO NOT need to have a DocuSign account in order to view and sign a document that is sent to them.

 

When a document needs your signature, you will receive an email.

 

NOTE: The email received will be an external email.

 

 

 

  1. Open the email

  2. Click on "Review Document".

  3. Wait for DocuSign to launch

  4. Open the Home screen

  5. Locate the top menu to find the action notification

  6. Click on the notification

     

  7. Wait for the manage tab to open

  8. Navigate to "Action Required" to find a record for the document

  9. Wait for the record of the document to display

     

  10. Click the "Sign" button to view and sign the document

     

    NOTE: The document will open but will be grayed out.

     

  11. Click "Continue" to fully open and sign the document OR

    • Click "Other Actions" if you want to

      • Print and Sign the document

      • Assign it to someone elese

      • Decline to sign it

     

    NOTE: If "Continue" is clicked, the Document will open fully and allow you to sign it.

     

  12. Click the yellow "Initial" icon to initial where required

  13. Click the yellow "Sign" icon to sign where required.

  14. Enter the required information in any red box

     

  15. Click the "Initial" and "Sign" button

  16. Enter the information, initials, signature, and information to appear in the document

  17. Click "FINISH" at the top or bottom of the page

     

  18. Navigate to your "Completed" documents section under the "Manage" drop-down

  19. Download or printed using the button and drop-down on the right of the "Manage" drop-down.

     

    NOTE: Once you have completed signing the document, the sender will be notified that the document has been signed.
