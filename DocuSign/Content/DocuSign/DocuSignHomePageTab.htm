DocuSign Home Page Tab

When launching DocuSign, the Home page will be displayed. Some items to note when viewing the DocuSign home page.

 

 

 

  1. View your last six months of document history in the top dashboard

     

  2. View your signature and optional photo in the upper left

     

    NOTE: Signature and photos can be modified from the "Setting Your Signature and Profile" section.

     

  3. View help information links at the bottom of the screen for DocuSign help

     

  4. Add documents to the middle of the page that you want to send for signatures
