Setting Your Signature and Profile

 

The Profile page is used to customize your DocuSign application.

 

This includes:

 

  • Entering personal information

  • Controlling Security preferences

  • Working with connected apps

  • Setting or modifying signature

  • Working with stamps

  • Setting language and location preference

 

 

  1. Access DocuSign profile by clicking the icon containing the users initials in the top right corner

  2. Click "Manage Profile"

     

  3. Set your name, email address, title, and contact information within the Personal tab

     

  4. Click the camera icon to add your photo next to your name and email address

     

    NOTE: The settings on the "Privacy & Security" tab are locked by the administrator. When accessing this tab, you may be prompted to re-enter your log in information.

     

    NOTE: The "Connected Apps" tab will be used in the future for applications that will have access to DocuSign.

     

  5. Create and modify your signatures, initials, and stamps within the "Signatures" tab

  6. Click "+Add New" to add a signature

  7. Click the "Edit" button to modify an existing signature

     

  8. Click the "CHOOSE" tab once the "Edit Signature" window opens

     

  9. Click the "Draw" tab to draw a signature using your mouse or other drawing device

     

  10. Click the "Upload" tab to upload an existing signature or initials file if you have one

     

  11. Add any custom stamps you may have using the "Stamps" tab

     

  12. Use the "Language and Region" tab to set your desired language and location

     

  13. Select "My Preferences" from the profile drop-down to set your general settings

     

  14. Wait for the screen to show a summary of your settings.

     

  15. Access more advanced settings using the side menu on the popup
