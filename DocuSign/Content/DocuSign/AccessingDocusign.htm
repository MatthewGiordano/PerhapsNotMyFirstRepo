Accessing Docusign

 

DocuSign is a web based application accessed through Okta.

 

NOTE: Users may be automatically logged out of DocuSign after a period of inactivity and will need to log back in.

 

  1. Launch a browser

  2. Click on the Okta icon

     

  3. Click on DocuSign in the application list

     

    NOTE: If you do not see the DocuSign app listed, log out of Okta and then proceed to log back in. If the DocuSign application is still not listed, submit a Service Request through ServiceNow.

     

  4. Ensure the correct email address has been entered

     

  5. Click "Continue"

  6. Enter your Martin Marietta password

  7. Click "Use Company Login"

     

  8. Log in with your company username and password

     

    NOTE: The DocuSign Home page will be displayed. It will show document history for the last 6 moths, and display the selected electronic signature. To set or Modify signatures, navigate to Setting Your Signature and Profile (Opens a new tab).
